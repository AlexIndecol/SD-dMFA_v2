# lifetime_mean_years.csv

Mean lifetime (years) per region/material/end-use.
- Columns: r,m,j,value
- Unit: years
- This file is OPTIONAL if you provide lifetime_lognormal_mu and lifetime_lognormal_sigma directly.
- Prefilled (where available) from MISO2 supporting information Table SI-21 for Zn/Ni/Sn (non-construction categories). Construction is left blank by design.
